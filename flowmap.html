Assignment 7 FLow map of Migration between Cities in Indonesia

I chose the divergent color scheme to exemplify the difference in flow magnitude between the largest flows and the smallest flows. It will also exemplify how there are very few flows in the larger categories and the vast majority of flows are in the bottom two categories. In addition to the color scheme, the thickness of the flow line also depicts the magnitude of the migration value. I chose the cured flow paths because it reduced the overlapping of flow lines and enhanced the readability of flow directions.

When looking at the flow pattern of migration between cities in Indonesia, there are a few noticeable patterns. First, there is a large amount of migration between four cities on the main islands of Sumatra and Java. Outside of these migration flows, all other flows are lower than 60,000 people. Furthermore, the majority of the flows are between the Islands of Sumatra, Java, and Borneo. Along with this, there are only 5 flows within the top 100 that either originate or end in the island of New Guinea.
